Ongoing Projects

Project Meridian — Variable Star Photometry
ACTIVE

Systematic long-baseline photometric monitoring of 62 variable stars across RR Lyrae, Cepheid, and semi-regular classes. Data submitted monthly to the AAVSO International Database. Members participate remotely from their own setups — each contributing nightly measurements that build our multi-year baseline dataset.

Project Horizon — Exoplanet Transit Search
ONGOING

Coordinated multi-site transit observations of known hot Jupiter systems from the ExoFOP catalog. Our distributed membership gives us simultaneous sky coverage across hemispheres — critical for capturing complete transit light curves that single sites often miss due to weather or daytime.

Solar Cycle Monitoring Programme
ACTIVE

Daily solar imaging and sunspot counting contributing to the SIDC World Sunspot Number dataset. Running continuously since 2021 with a 97.3% daily coverage rate — a figure made possible only by our globally distributed membership.

Comet C/2023 P1 Nishimura — Observing Campaign
COMPLETE

A 3-week coordinated imaging campaign capturing the passage of comet Nishimura. 847 images collected from 14 member setups across 6 countries, processed into a time-lapse sequence featured in Sky & Telescope online.
