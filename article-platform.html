AstroHaven's Collaboration Platform Phase 2: What's New

By: Technical Team·5 min read·Technology

After six months of development, Phase 2 of our custom online collaboration platform is live — bringing synchronised multi-site observation sessions and real-time data annotation to all members.

The Problem We Solved

When AstroHaven began, our members coordinated through a combination of Discord voice channels, shared spreadsheets, and email threads for data submission. Phase 2 goes substantially further than our original solution.

Phase 2 headline features: Synchronised multi-site imaging · Real-time shared annotation canvas · Automated alert routing · Voice coordination for 200+ participants · Integrated ephemeris calculator · Direct ExoFOP submission pipeline

Synchronised Multi-Site Imaging

The headline feature of Phase 2 is synchronised multi-site imaging — the ability for multiple members running supported telescope control software to receive and execute the same observation script simultaneously, with timing synchronised to GPS time via NTP.
