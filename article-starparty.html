Event Report · February 10, 2026

Winter Virtual Star Party Sets Attendance Record with 340 Viewers

By: Outreach Team·6 min read·Community

AstroHaven's annual Winter Star Party exceeded all previous records, with 340 attendees from 28 countries joining us across three consecutive evenings.

Three Nights, 28 Countries, One Sky

The 2026 Winter Star Party ran from February 7–9, live-streamed on our YouTube channel. Each evening began at 19:00 UTC and ran until the early hours, with different regions of the sky highlighted as the Earth rotated and different member telescopes came online.

Event by numbers: 340 total viewers · 28 countries represented · 47 first-time stargazers · 12 member telescopes · 4,200 chat messages

The 47 First-Timers

Perhaps the most meaningful number from this event was 47 — the number of viewers who identified themselves as having never engaged with astronomy before attending the Star Party. Of these, 23 indicated they were considering joining AstroHaven, and 11 had already submitted membership applications within 48 hours.
