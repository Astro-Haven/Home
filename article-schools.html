Eyes on the Universe Programme Reaches 1,000 Students

By: Outreach Division·7 min read·Education

AstroHaven's school outreach programme, "Eyes on the Universe," has now engaged over 1,000 students across 14 schools in 9 countries.

The Programme's Origins

Eyes on the Universe began in March 2022 as an informal initiative by Amina Osei. The first session was a 45-minute live Q&A with a single secondary school class in Ireland, sharing telescope views of the Moon.

Programme statistics (Dec 2025): 14 partner schools · 9 countries · 1,047 students reached · 89% of participating teachers reported increased science engagement · 23 students have since joined AstroHaven

Impact Beyond the Classroom

Three universities have reached out to explore partnership opportunities. And 23 students — some as young as 16 — have joined AstroHaven as members and are now contributing to our research programmes.
